## Presentation Tips

### For Each Slide:
1. **Start with the visual** - Let the chart tell the story
2. **State the research question** - What were we trying to learn?
3. **Present key findings** - What did we discover?
4. **Provide context** - Why does this matter?
5. **Actionable insight** - What should we do about it?

### Talking Points Structure:
```
"Let me show you [TOPIC]..."
"As you can see in this chart, [KEY FINDING]..."
"This is significant because [BUSINESS IMPACT]..."
"Based on this, we recommend [ACTION]..."
```

### Handling Questions:
- Refer to PROJECT_ANALYSIS_REPORT.md for detailed data
- All metrics are in the appendix
- Charts show exact values on data labels
- Ready to drill down into any demographic or category
