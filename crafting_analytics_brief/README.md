## üìã Analytics Brief Structure

### 1. Business Story
**What relevant actionable segments exist?**
- Electric Utilities (Primary Target)
- Oil & Gas
- Renewable Energy
- Nuclear Power

**Which ones should we pursue and why?**
- **Focus: Electric Utilities**
  - Proven ROI with existing customers
  - Regulatory pressure for grid modernization
  - Large addressable market ($260M+ U.S. substations alone)
  - Strong reference customers

### 2. Customer Story

**Target Audience**
- Asset management directors (utilities with 500+ employees)
- Operations Technology (OT) managers
- Safety & compliance officers
- Geographic focus: North America (expanding to Europe)

**Current Marketing Efforts**
- Industry Events (DistribuTECH, POWER-GEN)
- Direct Sales (enterprise account teams)
- Partner Network (Siemens, IFS system integrators)
- Case Studies (National Grid, Dominion success stories)
- Technical Webinars (ROI workshops, autonomous inspection demos)

**Recommended Focus Changes**
- Increase Orbit software adoption (currently geo-limited to North America)
- Develop pilot-to-fleet conversion programs
- Build vertical-specific use case packages
- Expand to Europe (Orbit cloud availability)

**Action Plan**
- **Now (Q1 2025)**:
  - Launch Utility ROI Calculator
  - Expand Orbit to Europe
  - Create $95K substation inspection starter package
  - LinkedIn thought leadership campaign
- **Later (Q2-Q4 2025)**:
  - Partner certification program
  - Spot-as-a-Service leasing model
  - Predictive maintenance AI for Orbit
  - Customer success community

### 3. Testing and Learning Plan

**What should we study further?**
- Pilot-to-fleet conversion rate drivers
- Orbit adoption barriers
- Competitive win/loss analysis
- Channel effectiveness analysis

**What should we try?**
- A/B test ROI messaging
- Partner co-marketing pilot with Siemens
- Free 90-day Orbit trial
- Intimate utility roundtable events

**Data Needs**
- Current Gaps:
  - Orbit adoption rate
  - Average fleet size per customer
  - Sales cycle duration
  - Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
  - Lead source attribution
- Recommended Collection:
  - CRM enhancement (vertical/use case tagging)
  - Orbit telemetry tracking
  - Win/loss interviews
  - Website analytics with UTM tracking
  - Post-purchase surveys (90-day, 1-year)

### 4. Purchase Process

**B2B Enterprise Funnel Stages**
1. **Awareness** - Industry events, website/SEO, LinkedIn, trade publications
2. **Interest** - Webinars, case studies, demo requests, email campaigns
3. **Consideration** - Partner network, direct sales, technical demos
4. **Decision** - Pilot programs, proposals, negotiations
5. **Post-Purchase** - Fleet expansion, Orbit adoption, customer success

**Key Metrics by Stage**
- Awareness: Booth visits, organic traffic, LinkedIn engagement, PR mentions
- Interest: Webinar attendance, case study downloads, demo request rate
- Consideration: Joint opportunities, discovery meetings, on-site demos
- Decision: Pilot success rate, proposal-to-close rate, contract value
- Post-Purchase: Pilot-to-fleet conversion, Orbit activation, NPS, upsell rate

**North Star Metrics**
- Pipeline Velocity (days in each stage)
- Demo-to-Close Rate
- Fleet Expansion Rate (robots added per customer annually)
- Orbit Attach Rate (% of Spot sales with Orbit)
